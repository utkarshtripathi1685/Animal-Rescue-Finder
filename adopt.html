Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for adopting a pet?

Requirements may vary by rescue organization but generally include being at least 18 years old, having a stable living situation, and being able to provide proper care for the pet.

How much does it cost to adopt a pet?

Adoption fees vary by organization and type of pet but typically range from $50 to $300. This fee often covers initial veterinary care, spaying/neutering, and microchipping.

Can I adopt if I rent my home?

Yes, but you'll need to provide proof that your landlord allows pets. Some rescues may contact your landlord to confirm.

How long does the adoption process take?

The process can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, depending on the organization and how quickly you can complete the necessary steps.
